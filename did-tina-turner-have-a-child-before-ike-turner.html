Tina Turner is an American-born Swiss singer and actress. tina was widely referred to as the “Queen of Rock ‘n’ Roll”, she rose to prominence as the lead singer of the Ike and Tina Turner before launching a successful career as a solo performer. Turner began her career with Ike Turner’s Kings of Rhythm in 1957.

Tina Turner was born on November 26, 1939 (age 82 years), in Nutbush, Tennessee, United States. She’s been married twice in her lifetime, she got married to her first husband Ike Turner in 1962 and divorced in 1978. Her second husband Erwin Bach got married in 2013.
