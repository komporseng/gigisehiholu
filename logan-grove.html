Logan Grove also known as Logan Parker Grove is an American actor, voice actor and model. As of 2023, Logan Grove’s net worth is $5 million. He is known for portraying Jason in the comedy family film “Arwin!” (2007) and Gumball Watterson in the animation short adventure tv series “The Amazing World of Gumball” (2011 – 2019).

He is the recipient of the BTVA Voice Acting Award for Best Vocal Performance by a Child (2012) and BTVA People’s Choice Voice Acting Award for Best Male Vocal Performance by a Child (2013).
