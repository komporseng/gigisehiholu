Who Is RHOP Charrisse Jackson-Jordan Daughter, Skylar Jordan?

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Remember Skylar Jordan, Charrisse Jackson-Jordan’s daughter from the August 2018 ep of Real Housewives of Potomac? Well, the then-freshman featured on RHOP alongside her mother has now graduated from Spelman College.

But that’s not the only thing you missed about her. Keep reading this Skylar Jordan Bio to learn everything about Charrisse’s daughter.

Meet Skylar Jordan, RHOP Star Charrisse Jackson-Jordan Daughter

Skylar Jordan and her mother Charrisse Jackson-Jordan are more friends than mom-daughter.

According to Skylar, she even often forgot that her mother was not her age, and so did Charrisse. However, for the RHOP star forgetting her age was international.

Wishing Skylar the best and confessing her wish to be young (prior to Skylar’s graduation day), Charrisse wrote on IG, “Can’t believe my baby and our besties (okay her besties- I’m always trying to be young 😂) are graduating next month.”

If you’ve been a long-time fan of Real Housewives of Potomac then you should know that Skylar joined Spelman College back in 2018. She was featured being dropped off by her mother at the college then.

Furthermore, before Skylar headed back to Potomac, Charrisse made sure that her daughter’s dorm room was nothing short of perfect. A white, blush, and gold color schemed dorm room even had a pair of super fluffy-looking slippers to match Skylar’s blush accent pillow and rug.

But this wasn’t the first time Charrisse spoiled her daughter. The RHOP star taking Skylar on dates and buying her the clothes she desired is nothing new to viewers.

To summarize, Charrisse loved Skylar to the moon. She was there to drop Skylar off at school and was there when she graduated. “I am probably the proudest mother alive to have a daughter that is so caring, intelligent, spiritual, has high standards and ambitions, and is overall a good person that makes loving her so easy,” the RHOP star praised her daughter on her graduation day.

Does Skylar Jordan Have A Sibling?

Yes, Skylar Jordan has two older siblings named Jackson (Skylar’s firstborn, birthday: March 21) and Justin.

Her brother, Jackson was a basketball athlete who played for Southern New Hampshire University.

Also, Jackson was often the star of Skylar’s socials. From dancing and lipsyncing on TikTok videos to FB appreciation posts, Jackson has graced his sister’s socials.

Here’s his IG @jacksonvljordan.

As for Skylar’s father, he is Eddie Jordan, a former basketball player for Rutgers (1973-1977).

Sadly, Skylar’s parents divorced in 2017 — after 20 years of togetherness. Why? Well, Eddie made it clear that if Charrisse was going to join Real Housewives of Potomac, they would get a divorce. And Charrisse did!

“Needless to say, I took that as my marriage was done. So, I said, well, I might as well do the show,” the star explained her decision.

Skylar Jordan Job

Skylar Jordan is a future dermatologist. Though she might have just graduated from Spelman College (with an undergraduate research assistant degree) now, someday she hopes to become a skin doctor who “caters to and researches black/ melanated skin.”

Also, working on her career, Skylar has already presented her findings at research conferences regionally and nationally.

Furthermore, she has also started an IG skincare blog @skinbysv where she shared and explained the proven information she discovered in a simplified way. She claimed that her findings didn’t “scare people away from skincare (since it can become overwhelming) and also highlight the differences in skincare practices for people of color.”

Here is the list of the jobs Skylar embarked upon before pursuing her career in dermatology — “product specialist” at Lululemon (from Oct 2020 – Feb 2021), “student shadow” at DuPont Aesthetic Institute (from June – August 2019) where she was able to interact with Dr. Dupont’s patients as well as view procedures if the patient consented, and as a night-shift mall employee (2020).

In her free time, Skylar loves shopping, making TikToks, visiting museums, and baking. Surprisingly, she’s an amazing baker as well.

Trivia: Skylar was part of the “Student of the Year” campaign in 2018 through the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society.
