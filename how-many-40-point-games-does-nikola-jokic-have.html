How many 40 point games does Nikola Jokic have?

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Nikola Jokic, often referred to as "the Joker," has established himself as one of the greatest players and centers in NBA history. Known for his versatility and exceptional skills on the court, Jokic has had an impressive number of games where he scored 40 or more points.

In his regular-season career, Jokic has recorded a total of 17 games with 40 or more points. These outstanding performances highlight his scoring prowess and ability to dominate the game.

When Jokic reaches the 40-point mark, he tends to excel in other statistical categories as well. On average, he contributes 12.2 rebounds, 7.8 assists, and 1.8 steals in these games. This demonstrates his impact as a well-rounded player who can contribute in multiple ways.

Jokic's shooting percentages in these games are remarkable. He boasts a shooting percentage of 63.6% from the field and an impressive 48.2% from the three-point line. These numbers further emphasize his exceptional shooting abilities, both inside and outside the arc.

In terms of total points scored in these 40-point games, Jokic has accumulated a staggering 736 points. This showcases his ability to consistently deliver high-scoring performances and contribute significantly to his team's success.

Furthermore, Jokic's impact on the game extends beyond individual statistics. He has an impressive plus-minus of +176 in these games, indicating the positive impact he has on his team's performance. This demonstrates his ability to elevate the play of his teammates and make a significant difference in the outcome of the game.

It is worth noting that Jokic's highest scoring game occurred against the Sacramento Kings, where he exploded for an astounding 50 points. This remarkable performance underscores his ability to rise to the occasion and deliver extraordinary performances when the team needs him the most.

In addition to his impressive scoring feats, Jokic has also achieved numerous accolades and records throughout his career. He has been selected as an NBA All-Star five times, named to the All-NBA Team on five occasions, and won the NBA Most Valuable Player Award twice. Jokic's contributions have not only earned him individual recognition but have also led the Denver Nuggets to their first NBA championship in the 2022-23 season.

Beyond the NBA, Jokic has represented the Serbian national team and won a silver medal at the 2016 Summer Olympics. His success and impact extend beyond the professional league, further solidifying his status as a basketball legend.

Nikola Jokic's 17 regular-season games with 40 or more points highlight his exceptional scoring ability and impact on the game.
