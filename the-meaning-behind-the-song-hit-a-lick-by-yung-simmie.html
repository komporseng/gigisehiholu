Now, let’s delve into the meaning behind the song. “Hit A Lick” is a track that showcases Yung Simmie’s raw and gritty lyrics, delivering a visceral experience to the listener. The lyrics depict a narrative of a person who is about to commit a robbery, referred to as “hitting a lick.” The phrase “hit a lick” is slang in the urban community, generally used to describe a successful theft or robbery.

Yung Simmie’s lyrics imbue a sense of confidence in his ability to execute the act, mentioning actions like wearing a ski mask and infiltrating someone’s house. The repetition of the chorus emphasizes his determination to carry out the robbery, where he also mentions stealing possessions and even taking someone’s life.

The song’s second verse maintains the same theme, with Yung Simmie adding more details to the narrative. He suggests that he is unafraid to engage in criminal activities to achieve his desired outcome, expressing his pursuit of financial gain and the willingness to eliminate obstacles. The line “I erase a nigga quick, like a pencil with that lead” metaphorically illustrates his readiness to eliminate anyone who stands in his way.

It is important to note that Yung Simmie’s lyrics in “Hit A Lick” reflect a storytelling element often found in hip-hop music. While the lyrics describe criminal actions, it is crucial to approach the song as a form of artistic expression and not as a celebration or endorsement of real-life illegal activities.
