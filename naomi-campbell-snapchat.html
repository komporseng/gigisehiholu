Naomi Campbell is a British model and actress, who after being discovered at the age of 15 has become one of the most recognisable faces in the world of fashion.

Over the years Naomi has walked in fashion shows for Gianni Versace, Diane Von Furstenberg, Azzedine Alaïa, Donna Karan and Isaac Mizrahi and graced the front cover of every fashion magazine possible, from Vogue to Marie Claire, Elle to Interview, Essence to Harper’s Bazaar.

Away from the world of fashion, Naomi has had a varied career, acting in several films and television shows including The Call, Prisoner of Love, Empire, Ugly Betty and Star.
