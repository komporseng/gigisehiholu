Genneya Walton Plays Chloe Barris in the Netflix Series Black AF aka #blackAF

Black AF, which is stylized as #blackAF, is a Netflix series created by Kenya Barris, who is also starring in the lead role. The single-camera sitcom was also known as #blackexcellence when it was announced in 2019. The series also features Rashida Jones in a leading role alongside Kenya Barris.

The new Netflix series is, “Inspired by Barris’ irreverent, highly flawed, unbelievably honest approach to parenting, relationships, race, and culture, #blackexcellence flips the traditional sitcom family on its head. Pulling back the curtain, the series uncovers and explores the messy, unapologetic and often hilarious world of what it means to be a “new money” Black family trying to get it right in a modern world where “right” is no longer a fixed concept,” according to Deadline.

Genneya Walton plays Chloe Barris in the series, the eldest of the Barris kids. She is 18 years old, extremely popular who is also entitled beyond measure. Black AF aka #blackAF will be available for streaming on 17 April 2020, only on Netflix, starring Kenya Barris, Rashida Jones, Genneya Walton, Iman Benson as Drea Baris, Scarlet Spenser as Izzy Barris, Justin Claiborne as Pops Barris, Ravi Cabot-Conyers as Cam Barris and Richard Gardenhire Jr. as Brooklyn Barris.

Genneya Walton Found Fame with the Role of Bryden Bandweth in the Series Project Mc2

As mentioned above, Genneya started to appear in front of the camera at the age of 12, the same time, she started taking acting classes in Brooke Byler Acting Studio. After playing the bully in the Disney Channel movie Isabella Dances Into the Spotlight, Genneya landed the role of Bryden Bandweth in the series Project Mc2.

The show was well-received by fans, and for 26 episodes, Genneya played the character of Bryden in the series. Even after the show was canceled fans clamored for more, to this day, there are fans of the show asking Genneya on her Instagram posts, whether new episodes of Project Mc2 will be made available.

Genneya Walton also appeared in the series Extant with Halle Berry and the TV adaptation of the School of Rock. She appeared in one episode of Criminal Minds and also in two episodes of the hit series 9-1-1. Recently the actress appeared in a single episode of medical drama series The Resident, and now she will play Chloe in the Netflix series Black AF.
