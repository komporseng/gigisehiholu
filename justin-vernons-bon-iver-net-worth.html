Justin Vernon Wiki Biography

Justin DeYarmond Edison Vernon, born on April 30, 1981, in Eau Claire, Wisconsin USA, is widely known as a musician – singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. He is the founder and frontman of the indie-rock band Bon Iver and also the music producer of the band’s albums. His career has been active since the early 2000s.

Justin Vernon’s overall net worth is estimated at $5 million, acquired through his music career, during which he has released several albums.

Justin Vernon spent his childhood in Eau Claire, attending elementary school and Memorial High School. Later, beginning in 1999, he studied Religious and Women’s Studies at the University of Wisconsin – Eau Claire. He was a good student and participated in a student exchange program, spending one semester in Ireland. Soon after, his focus moved to music, and he ‘found’ himself.

Vernon has been involved in music since his college days, forming two bands, DeYarmond Edison and Mount Vernon. Mount Vernon was short-lived, but DeYarmond Edison was quite successful in his hometown. After a few years, Vernon moved the band to North Carolina to expand his career. The band managed to release their first self-titled album in 2004 and a year later, their second release, “Silent Signs”. However, the band eventually broke up due to internal conflicts.

Nevertheless, Vernon returned to music soon after, forming Bon Iver, which means “good winter” in French. The band’s first release, “For Emma, Forever Ago” in 2007, struck a chord with the public and attracted the attention of several record labels. Justin signed with the independent record label “Jagjaguwar”, and the album was eventually certified Gold, selling over 500,000 copies. This increased Vernon’s net worth and inspired him to continue writing music.

The band’s next release was the 2009 EP titled “Blood Bank”, which reached the 1st place on the UK Indie Chart, contributing to Vernon’s net worth through its sales. In 2012, Bon Iver released their latest album, “Bon Iver, Bon Iver”, receiving highly positive reviews and debuting in 2nd place on the US Billboard 200 chart.

Justin has won several prestigious awards during his career with Bon Iver, including the Best New Artist award in 2012, and Best Alternative Music Album for the “Bon Iver, Bon Iver” release.

In addition to his successful career with Bon Iver, Justin is also a member of the bands Volcano Choir and Gayngs, further contributing to his net worth.
