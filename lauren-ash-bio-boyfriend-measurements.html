Lauren Ash Has Worked As A Writer

The dexterous actress who has gained wide recognition for her roles in hit series like Superstore and Super Fun Nights has also plied her trade in script-writing. In 2012, she penned down seven episodes of Trashy Affair, a TV Mini-Series. And similarly, in 2018, she wrote one episode of Superstore.

Her other writing credits include Video on Trial, Punched Up and Facebook of Revelation short film series. In addition to that, Lauren Ash also provided the soundtrack for two episodes of the TV series, Super Fun Night and one episode of Hollywood Game Night.

Lauren Ash Has Won a Handful of Accolades

Lauren Ash has claimed multiple prestigious accolades over the course of her career. She has received the Canadian Award for Best Performance by a Female twice in 2012, and also the Canadian Comedy Award for Best Female Improviser twice in the years 2006 and 2007. She is additionally a recipient of the Canadian Screen Awards and ACTRA Toronto Award nomination.

Health Complications

The talented actress who is now a vegetarian was slightly overweight in early 2015. She noticed that she had gathered 30lb within six months as she began experiencing health issues such as premenstrual syndrome, skin allergies, and irregular menstruation during such time.

Trying to find a cure to the health problems, she visited a hospital and was diagnosed with Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), a hormonal disorder causing enlarged ovaries with small cysts on the outer edges. Afterward, she went under the knife in 2016 to remove the cysts. Although the condition can be managed through medications, it can’t be cured. As such, Lauren has been placed on medication and supplements. She is currently an advocate for PCOS.
