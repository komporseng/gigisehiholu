  • Mark Yezak Job

Mark Yezak, popularly known as Dr. Mark Yezak, is a chiropractor. He has been the founder, chiropractic director, and chief of staff at Houston Spine & Rehabilitation Centers for ages now. Mark at the same time also is a published author of research papers, books, and journal entries. The patriarch earned his bachelor’s degree and Doctor of Chiropractic degree at Texas Chiropractic College in Pasadena. Following that, he completed a pre-medicine program at Sam Houston State University in Huntsville, Texas. Then, afterward, having earned his chiropractic degree, he furthered his education through training at the Texas Back Institute in Plano.
