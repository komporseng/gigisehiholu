The Fashion Star judge has come under fire from critics about her weight gain during pregnancy. All expecting mothers get a little bigger, but Jessica may be in dangerous territory not just for her health, but for her baby’s as well.

“Her doctors have told her to expect a 10 lb baby,” Dr. Tara Solomon, owner at The Women’s Wellness Center of South Florida, told HollywoodLife.com on March 27. “The problem with large babies is that they can have respiratory problems and problems maintaining their glucose or sugar levels. I usually try to keep my patients at a 25 lb weight gain maximum in order to prevent a possible traumatic delivery with a large child. A pregnant woman should be eating about 1200 calories a day to keep up with baby’s nutritional needs. She has gone overboard for sure.”

Whoa! No need to get violent, Mama Bear.
