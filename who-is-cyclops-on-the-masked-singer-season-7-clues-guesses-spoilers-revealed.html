Who is Cyclops on The Masked Singer Season 7? Clues, Guesses, &amp; Spoilers Revealed!

Publish date: 2024-07-19

The Masked Singer is back!

Season seven of the hit mystery singing competition series returned on Wednesday night (March 9) on FOX.

This season, the 15 contestants in the competition will be split into three teams known as “The Good,” “The Bad”, and “The Cuddly.”

This is the first season since the fourth season not to feature wild card contestants, and the first to feature teams. Nick Cannon will be hosting the season.

During the episode, Cyclops, from Team Bad, took to the stage for a performance of “My Sacrifice” by Creed.

Click inside to check out all of the clues and guesses for Cyclops…

FIRST PERFORMANCE CLUES:

– His clue-mmercial is for “cy-drops,” eyedrops for bloodshot cyclops eyes. It comes in a “4 oz” can

– A compass points South East

– A framed photo of a turtle in a cowboy hat is shown

– Says, “Growing up, I connected with monsters more than heroes. I was the kind of kid who was disappointed at the end of Beauty and the Beast when he turned handsome”

PANEL GUESSES: William Zabka of Kobra Kai, Danny McBride, Rob Dyrdek
