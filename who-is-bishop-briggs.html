Who is Bishop Briggs? Reveal the Journey of Someone Else Singer

Publish date: 2024-06-09

Famous as Bishop Briggs is one the most famous singer in British America. Her real name is Sarah Grace McLaughlin. In 2018 she debuted her singing career with her album Church of Scars and her single “River” reached number three on the US Alternative Chart.

Let’s know more about her early life, Career and other details. 

Who is Bishop Briggs?

Sarah was born on 18 July 1992, in London. Her parent belongs to Bishopbriggs that’s why she knows as Bishop Briggs. They moved to Tokyo at an age of four and there she perform for the first time her parents understand what she wants to become in the future.

She start composing her own song when she was just seven years old and perform in front of her family. She sifted in Hong Kong and stay there until she complete 18. During her school, she participates in talent shows.

She completes her graduate degree from Hong Kong International School and moved to Los Angele to join the Musician Institute.

After moving to Los Angeles she starts her songwriting profession and in 2015 she released her first single “Wild Horses.” But she gets more fame via Shazam. After her commercial Acura her song famous worldwide and reached Billboard Alternative Song CHart among the top 30.

Her next single “River” was released in 2016 and is a commercial success. Her third single was “The Way I Do.” She gave an opening performance for the Coldplay tour and then also performed the opening act Kaleo. She also appeared on television in 2016, The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon.

Her fourth single was “Pray” and her next is “Be Your Love.” Bishop also gave her presence at Panorama Festival 2017 and also perform at 2017’s Coachella. She comes back in the soundtrack of xXx: Return of Xander Cage with her song “Mercy”.

In 2018 she released her first album which is Church of Scars. In November 2019 her next album titled Champion was released.
