Cinthya Carmona- Biography

Cinthya Carmona is a Venezuelan actress best known for her portrayal of Brandie in the television series East Los High. She is quite adaptable and talented, and she has made a thriving career for herself. She is about to reach new heights in her career, beginning with the premiere of her new show Pretty Smart.

Cinthya Carmona- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings

Cinthya Carmona was born in Venezuela on December 27, 1990, and is of mixed heritage. Her ancestors are from Venezuela and Columbia, and she is known to be a Christian. Cinthya is a high school graduate who also attended the Speiser Sturges Acting Studio in Los Angeles for her acting and theatre training. In addition, she studied acting and drama in New York.

Cinthya Carmona- Professional Career

Cinthya began her acting career as a theatre artist in New York, where she appeared in productions and plays such as Barefoot in the Park and The Glass Menagerie and frequently played at the Mainstreet Playhouse Theatre. She began acting in films and television series after finishing her theatre studies. She began starring in minor roles in numerous shows and films in 2009. In 2012, she starred as Sloane Peterson in the short series Super 35, as Lupe in True Justice, and as the Fan in Success Driven. Her acting career took off in 2016 when she landed the role of Brandie in the sitcom East Los High. Cinthya Carmona earned a lot of attention for her talent as Brandie, and she soon landed another job as Sophie Cardona in the show Greenhouse Academy.

Cinthya Carmona has also appeared in the films Ambushed (2013), Badge of Honor (2015), Power (2015), Private Space (2015), and Rush Hour (2016). She was most recently seen in the films The Fix and Skin in the Game in 2019. In 2020, she was cast in the film Tax Collector as Alexis, and the same year, she also appeared in the films Deputy as Nurse Julie, A Dark Foe as Maria, and Painter as Lupe. She is currently cast as Solana in the Netflix original sitcom Pretty Smart, for which she has received high accolades even before the show has aired. She will also appear in two new films, Repeater, and Reefa.
