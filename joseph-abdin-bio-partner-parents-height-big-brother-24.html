Meet Joseph Abdin! Bio, Partner, Parents, Height

Publish date: 2024-07-27

Joseph Abdin is one of the 16 contestants competing for the grand prize of $750 thousand. Lawyer by profession, he is expected to perform very well in the show.

Now, let’s learn more about his partner, parents, height, and his social media reach. Below we attempt to cover it all based on all the information we’ve gathered.

Joseph Abdin On Big Brother 24

Before entering the Big Brother house, Joseph Abdin talked to Parade.com. He shared that the best advice he got before competing was “put yourself out there,” “try new things,” and “take risks”. He shared with the outlet that his fellow house guests would likely perceive him as “all brawn and no brain”. Also, he added that it was the theme he got all of his life.

Joseph added, “Most likely, they’re gonna look at me as just a physical threat and nothing more”.

We know that the only way to survive and have the possibility to win the show is to have a strong alliance. In this aspect, Joseph knows that he doesn’t want to be around “untrustworthy,” “unstrategic,” and “loud” people. He added he doesn’t want an alliance with, “People who aren’t moving stealthily, just moving to move with no strategy behind their game”.

Joseph also added something others won’t enjoy about him. He added, “I eat a lot of food. I’m definitely gonna eat! (Laughs.) To the point where they’re not gonna be able to eat as much. I’m also always working out, whether it’s yoga, meditation, or weightlifting. I don’t really sleep, so it can be annoying for other people if they want to get some rest and I’m doing something”.

As for flipping vote or going with house alliance, he prefers to go with the latter. He added that there’s only one winner and one can’t reach to the final with a proper alliance so working with an alliance is a key to his longevity in the show.

Fun Fact — Joseph was a replacement houseguest for Marvin Achi, who was slated to be on Big Brother 24 (US), but was dropped from the cast a day before the season premiere.

Joseph Abdin Job

According to Joseph Abdin’s LinkedIn, he worked as Legal Extern In-House Counsel at NextEra Energy, Inc. He joined the company in January 2021 and worked there as an in-house corporate counsel.

Previously, he served in Florida Supreme Court as a Florida Supreme Court Extern from May 2021 to August 2021. In August 2021, he took over the FSU College Of Law’s Instagram page to show a day in his life at the court. The video is available on FSU’s YouTube channel.

In their collegiate years, Joseph also indulged in competitive powerlifting. In 2016, he competed in three events 2016 USA Powerlifting Collegiate Nationals (Squat, Deadlift) and Orlando Open Championships (Bench Press).

Speaking on his academic qualifications, Joseph earned his bachelor’s degree in business administration. He studied business administration and management and graduated in 2018. He had double majored in Business Management & International Business.

As for his J.D., Joseph earned it in 2021 from Florida State University College of Law. He completed his Florida Bar Exam in February 2022.

Joseph Abdin Parents

Joseph Abdin was born to his father Hassan Abdin.

Born in February 1960, Hassan is 62 years old in 2022. According to his social media posts, Hassan came from a big family and has four brothers.

According to his LinkedIn, Hassan is currently based in Pompano Beach, Florida, and owns Total Food Mart. Moreover, he also owned Suniland Meat & Fish, Inc. a company based in North Lauderdale. The company was registered on 25 July 2002.

Hassan himself is a father of at least four children. Besides Joseph, he has two daughters Daniella Abdin and Danna Abdin. Not much is known about his second son.

Daniella Abdin is 26 years old born on 9 October 1995. She is a licensed real estate agent working in Parkland, Florida. She has a total experience of 3 years in the real estate business.

Moreover, Daniella is currently working for Coldwell Banker. She is also the co-founder of Acton Academy. Danna Abdin graduated from Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School. She is 23 years old and attended the University of Florida.
