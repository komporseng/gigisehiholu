Heart Of Worship Lyrics

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Heart of Worship by Matt Redman is an iconic anthem that transcends time, inviting listeners to a place of sincere devotion and stripped-down worship.

Released in 1999, this timeless Christian song continues to resonate with its powerful message, reminding believers of the essence of worship and the importance of laying aside the trappings of performance in order to seek God’s hear.
