A Look Into Robin Roberts And Amber Laign Relationship

Publish date: 2024-09-08

American journalist Robin Roberts announced intentions to marry her longtime girlfriend, Amber Laign, this year. On Good Morning America on January 2, the 62-year-old star disclosed to motivational speaker Gabrielle Bernste in that she was “hesitating” since she had not yet announced the news.

She stated:

“I’ve decided to be married. This year, we’re getting married.”

She went on to say that the couple had to postpone their wedding in the past because Laign grew unwell after being diagnosed with breast cancer.

She stated:

“It was something we had discussed but had put off because she got unwell, but… it is saying yes to that, and that new chapter.”

Amber Laign and Robin Roberts met on a blind date.

Robin Roberts and Amber Laign met for the first time on a blind date put up by mutual friends in 2005. They were secretly dating until 2013 when Roberts revealed his sexual orientation in a Facebook post. The Good Morning America presenter posted a photo of herself playing with a puppy and expressed gratitude to her family and friends for their support as she recovered after a bone marrow transplant.

She stated:

“I am thankful to God, my physicians, and nurses for my improved health. I am thankful to my donor, Sally-Ann, for providing me with the gift of life. As we prepare to ring in the new year, I am thankful for my complete family, my long-term partner, Amber, and our friends.”

Roberts confessed on The Ellen DeGeneres Show in 2014 why she had not spoken publicly about Amber despite the fact that they had been dating since 2005.

She said, ”

“Not everyone wants to be the center of attention. She’s been there for me every step of the way, and she’s really supportive.”

Amber Laign is a massage therapist and the co-founder of Plant Juice Oils, a women’s wellness product line. Robin Roberts and Amber have two homes: the host’s opulent Manhattan apartment near her GMA studio, and a Connecticut property where they live while Roberts is not filming for the program. Amber, whose Instagram account is secret, came to the site in 2020 and published a photo reminiscing about their first meeting in 2005. We went on another date shortly after, and I couldn’t believe how comfortable I felt with you as we talked our lives while drinking lychee and apple martinis.”

Amber and Robin are both cancer survivors.
