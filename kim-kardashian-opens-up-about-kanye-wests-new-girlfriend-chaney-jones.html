Kim Kardashian Opens Up About Kanye Wests New Girlfriend, Chaney Jones

Publish date: 2024-05-06

In a recent episode of Amanda Hirsch’s Not Skinny Not Fat podcast, Kim Kardashian finally shared her thoughts on her ex-husband Kanye West’s new girlfriend, Chaney Jones.

During the podcast, Kim addressed the resemblance between Jones and herself, acknowledging that many have labeled Jones as a Kim “lookalike.”

Kanye West was first seen with Jones in January while still in a relationship with Julia Fox. Kim revealed on the podcast that she holds no negative feelings towards Kanye’s dating life.

Hirsch pointed out the resemblance between the women Kanye has been involved with, suggesting that they seem to be fans of Kim or even dress like her. Kim responded that the similarity doesn’t bother her.

She expressed her desire for Kanye to be happy and shared that Jones “seems like the sweetest.” Kim emphasized that she genuinely wants Kanye to be happy, regardless of the circumstances.

Kim further commented on the impact of happiness on one’s life, work, and parenting, stating that as long as Kanye is happy, she supports his decisions.

Jones was also seen at West’s Donda 2 listening party, confirming speculation about their relationship. It was revealed in February that West had ended things with Fox and started a new relationship with Jones.
