Kenya Moore from RHOA Hugs Her Younger Brother in Cute Throwback Photo from Her Teenage Years

Publish date: 2024-04-25

"Real Housewives of Atlanta" star Kenya Moore took her followers on Instagram down memory lane with a lovely picture she shared, which showed her hugging her younger brother.

Kenya Moore shared a cute throwback photo of herself and her younger brother from back in the day. The picture showed Kenya sporting a purple-colored buttoned dress, while holding her little brother.

The television personality who was sitted wore a long purple dress and tilted her head to the side. Her brother, who was very much young then, looked shy as he stared at the camera.

Kenya Moore visiting BuzzFeed's "AM to DM" on November 4, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

Kenya's thick hair was in full display in the photograph. The star doesn't look to have changed so much from her teenage girl looks, a fact many of her fans who commented emphasized.

In her caption to the post, Kenya Moore clearly stated that the cute child in the picture wasn't her baby girl, Brooklyn, but her younger brother. She wrote:

"Nope that's not @thebrooklyndaly that's me and my little brother. I was about 13/14 years old."

Unsurprisingly, loads of comments followed Kenya's post, with many of her fans loving her hair, and also saying that the way she looks now is not so different from her looks as a young girl.

One fan was amazed at the mother of one's hair and told her that she should stop allowing people to play with her when it comes to her hair. Another fan said Kenya looked mature as a 13-year-old.

The little tot had a mischievous look on her face, and made her way out of her mom's patio, wearing an animal print dress.

A third fan said the 49-year-old still looked the same and is naturally beautiful, while a fourth person said that with all that hair, Kenya has always been good looking.

Kenya Moore is ever so proud of her one-year-old baby daughter, Brooklyn, and has many of her videos and pictures splashed over her Instagram page. One of Kenya's recent posts about her daughter was quite hilarious.

The video featured Brooklyn on a mission to escape from home to see her father, Marc Daly, who is in New York City. Kenya shared the video on her Instagram page on March 19.

The little tot had a mischievous look on her face, and made her way out of her mom's patio, wearing an animal print dress. When asked by her mother where she was headed to, Brooklyn muttered, "Daddy."

Kenya told her daughter that her daddy was in New York, with the adorable baby girl looking at her mother with shock written all over her face. Kenya managed to bring her back in, and narrated the incidence in her caption, with a pet dog also seen in the video, walking about.

However, Kenya's video derailed when her fans mentioned that Brooklyn didn't say "Daddy," but "Doggy" in the video, and that the television personality got the interpretation of her daughter's words wrong.

Other fans also believed that Kenya only wanted to mention Marc Daly, her estranged husband, in the video post. Whatever the case was, the fact remains that the video was filled with cuteness.
